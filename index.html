Coins are earned by selling crops, eggs and wine, completing deliveries, and logging in each day. Use them to buy new seeds, animals and truck upgrades.

How do deliveries work?+

Load your truck with produce, choose a destination on the map, and send it off. Better trucks and routes mean bigger rewards.

Can I upgrade trucks?+

Absolutely. Upgrade your truck's capacity and speed over time so each delivery earns you more.

Not at all. Harvest Haulers is designed to be picked up in minutes, with gentle tutorials guiding your very first harvest.

Will the game keep pestering me with notifications?+

No. You control your notification settings entirely, and the game is designed to be enjoyed at whatever pace suits you.
